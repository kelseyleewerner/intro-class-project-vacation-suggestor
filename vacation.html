Mount Everest Ski Resort

Ski the slopes of the planet's tallest mountain at the resort of the future! An epic experience awaits that more than makes up for the chilly weather: ride a pressure and temperature-controlled aeral tram to enjoy a view from the top or explore the lower peaks with a Sherpa guided tour.

Sydney Surf Escape

Endulge your adventurous side: take surf lessons and snorkle with sharks near Sydney's famous harbour. Feeling a little more tame? Explore all the city's shopping, dining, and cultural activities. Either way, be sure to pack plenty of SPF!
